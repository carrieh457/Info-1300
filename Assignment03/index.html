Cornell Fall

Cornell is gorgeous in the fall with reds, oranges, and yellows filling campus.Students can lay on the slope before it is carpeted with snow, go kayaking in Bebe lake, ortake a nice walk on the trails.

Cornell Clock Tower

The Cornell Clock Tower is Cornell's heartbeat in a way by marking each hour and chime concert. It is played by student chimemasters everyday at a certain time. There are 161 steps up to the top of the tower where you can get a gorgeous view of central campus.
